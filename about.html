Our bus company over the years:

Information on Our Buses

We have had trips to the Baltics since 1997. The bus operations were founded in 1988. We have two buses.

Travel Guarantee

Over the years we have had tourist trips to Norway, Denmark, Finland, Russia, Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Poland, Germany, Austria, France, and Spain. As well as group trips to the USA, both east and west coast. But now we focus mostly on Estonia.

Extra Info

We hold an International Traffic Permit, Travel Guarantee F tax and have the highest credit rating.
