FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When will the bill come?

Billing and payment

The bill usually comes between 4 and 3 weeks before departure We do not send partial payments, but the entire bill is sent at once It can also be sent out somewhat earlier if the trip is fully booked The bill also includes a travel program The bill and program are only sent to the person who has given the address at the time of booking An unpaid bill is not considered a cancellation, but the cancellation is not completed before you have received a cancellation notice from our office (see Travel Terms and Conditions) Bills are not sent out if we see that the number of participants is too few (under 10 people) Should the trip be canceled due to too few participants, the message will be sent no later than 2 weeks before the planned departure to the phone number you provided when booking

How can you pay?

Paying on the trip

On the ship: Can you pay with Master Card, Visa card, and EURO At the hotel: Can you pay with Master Card, Visa card and EURO The liquor store SuperAlko: Can you pay with Master Card, Visa card, and EURO Department stores: Master Card, Visa card and EURO Restaurants: Pay preferably in cash EURO but also Master Card, Visa card Markets: Can you only pay with EURO Tervis: Can you pay with Master Card, Visa card, EURO Exchange: Can you do with the driver up to 50 euros ATMs: Used Master Card, Visa card Available at the Tallink-Silja terminal Värtahamnen withdrawal in Swedish kronor The D-terminal in Tallinn withdrawals in EURO Many in both Tallinn and Pärnu City and Riga Withdrawal in EURO

Can the trip be cancelled?

Cancellation?

Should the trip be cancelled.. Please let us know if you have not received the bill and information about 10 days before departure Then you might have time to book another trip or move to another departure date that we know will go The bill will not be sent until we see that the trip will be completed, so you pay nothing until the bill and travel program reach you If the bill arrives earlier, the departure is guaranteed Many trips go, of course, but there may be dates that are not suitable for people to travel, so the bookings will be few A few times, the shipping company can charter the entire ship at short notice, causing the trip to fall because of this. In very extreme weather or ice conditions the trip can be canceled at very short notice but this has never happened to us since 1997 We hope you understand that a trip may be cancelled
