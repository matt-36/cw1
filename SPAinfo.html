What is Tervis?

Tervis Hälsospahotell is a recreation center with, among other things: Such as pain relief, stress relief and body care with the help of a doctor

Benefits of Tervis

TERVIS is one of the best SPA facilities in Estonia. With a lot of experience, a beautiful location by the sea and green areas around the hotel in the summer city, the festival city, the theater city of Pärnu.

Whats included

Included:
-18 treatments and 18 buffet meals including drinks at the hotel.
-The bus journey across Värmland, Örebro, Västerås and Enköping.
-The boat trip (no meals on the ship), the Tallinn-Pärnu bus trip and a tour of Pärnu. Access to pool and gym
